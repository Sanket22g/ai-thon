AI-THON 2026 is a premier hackathon where innovation meets intelligence. Teams will conceptualize, design, and present groundbreaking AI-powered solutions in a fast-paced, high-energy environment.

Whether you're building with Generative AI, deploying autonomous agents, fine-tuning LLMs, crafting Machine Learning models, or exploring Data Science — this is your arena to shine.
